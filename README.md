This is a Lyrion Music Server (LMS) plugin that will integrate the iBroadcast.com  personal music library with your LMS music library.
Allowing you to stream your online music library from your LMS music library.   https://ibroadcast.com

Broadcast allow you to upload your entire music collection and stream it online and to a number of different devices.
It is free to upload your music collection, the free service is fully functional, but streaming is limited to 128kbps.  
If you have an iBroadcast premium account you can stream at britrate of your choice including the original audio quality of the files you uploaded to your iBroadcast library.

You can install the plugin from the "Manage Plugins" settings of your LMS. You will find it in the 3rd Party plugins sections, under "Music Services"

Once Installed sign in on the iBroadcast settings page using your iBroadcast username/email and password and press the "sigin-in" button. This will retrieve an authentication token from iBroadcast for the plugin. Your sign-in credentials are not stored.

Once signed in, you can rescan your library (or choose to scan your online library only) and your iBroadcast music library will be scanned and imported into your library.
Playing an iBroadcast track will stream it from your online library.
If you have automatic check for changes selected on your Online Music Library settings, then the plugin will automatic update your library if your iBroadcast library has changed.
